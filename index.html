Show ACF Fields on Posts or Products

Overview

This repository provides a comprehensive solution to display Advanced Custom Fields (ACF) data on posts or products within your WordPress website. If you've been looking for an easy way to showcase additional information related to your posts or products, this plugin is exactly what you need!

Installation

  1. Clone this repository to your local environment or download the latest release.
  2. Upload the plugin files to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress dashboard by navigating to Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin.
  3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

Usage

Once the plugin is activated, you can easily configure which ACF fields you want to display on your posts or products. Head over to the plugin settings page, where you can enable or disable specific ACF fields and choose their position on the post or product page. The plugin seamlessly integrates into your website's backend, ensuring a user-friendly experience.

Features

1. Effortless ACF Integration

Say goodbye to complicated code or template modifications! Our plugin streamlines the process of integrating ACF fields onto your posts or products, saving you valuable time and effort.

2. Flexible Field Placement

Customize the display of ACF fields to suit your website's layout and design. Choose from various placement options, including before or after content, within specific sections, or even as an overlay.

3. Support for Different Field Types

No matter what type of data your ACF fields contain – text, images, dates, or more complex custom data – our plugin effortlessly handles them all. It ensures that your content is beautifully presented to engage your website visitors effectively.

4. Conditional Display

With our plugin, you have the power to conditionally show or hide ACF fields based on specific criteria, such as post categories, tags, or custom taxonomies. This feature empowers you to deliver personalized content tailored to your audience.

5. Performance Optimization

We understand the importance of website performance. Rest assured, our plugin has been built with optimization in mind, ensuring that displaying ACF fields doesn't slow down your website.

6. Seamless Compatibility

The plugin works harmoniously with popular page builders, custom themes, and various plugins, making it a seamless addition to your WordPress ecosystem.

Customization

While our plugin comes pre-configured with sensible defaults, we understand that every website is unique. You can easily customize the appearance and behavior of ACF fields by modifying the plugin's settings or extending its functionality using hooks and filters.
